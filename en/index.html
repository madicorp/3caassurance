Car insurance

In almost all markets, Motor Vehicle Insurance (VTM), also known as automobile insurance, accounts for the largest share of non-life insurance.

The "automobile" branch includes, on the one hand, the guarantee of civil liability arising from the use of the vehicle and, on the other hand, cover of the damage which this vehicle may suffer.

It should be noted that the regulations impose the obligation of insurance (Law n ° 7433 of 18/07/1974 instituting in the Senegal the obligation of insurance of the VTM, supplemented by the CIMA code in its book 2). However, the mandatory nature is limited to the RC component, but the majority of automobile policies also cover, in different ways, the damage suffered by the vehicle and its accessories, but also the person of the driver of the vehicle and third parties transported for bodily injury .

The fire warranty applies to damage caused by fire or explosions and is often accompanied by a theft warranty which covers the damage caused by the disappearance or attempted theft of the vehicle and its accessories.

The broken window warranty that supports the rear window, side windows and the socket clamp. Damage to the vehicle and its accessories can also be covered by a collision with another vehicle, a collision with a fixed or mobile body, or the payment of the vehicle (a guarantee known as a complete third party). You can also take a more limited guarantee - the third collision - that covers only damage caused by a collision with an identified pedestrian, vehicle or animal belonging to an identified person.

The CR is also accompanied by supplementary guarantees such as defense and recourse which is legal protection as well as the advance on appeal which constitutes a subrogation but also products of assistance such as towing, the replacement vehicle ...; The automobile multi-risk policy consolidates these guarantees.
